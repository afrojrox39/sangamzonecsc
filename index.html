Welcome To Sangam zone CSC

WELCOME TO CSC Common Service Centers (CSC) scheme is one of the mission mode projects under the Digital India Programme. CSCs are the access points for delivery of essential public utility services, social welfare schemes, healthcare, financial, education and agriculture services, apart from host of B2C services to citizens in rural and remote areas of the country. It is a pan-India network catering to regional, geographic, linguistic and cultural diversity of the country, thus enabling the Governments’ mandate of a socially, financially and digitally inclusive society. Below mentioned are services portifolio of CSCs.

● Aadhaar Card
● Pan Card
● Voter Card
● Aayushman Card
● Business to Citizen
● Financial Inclusion
● Education
● Agriculture
● Health Services
